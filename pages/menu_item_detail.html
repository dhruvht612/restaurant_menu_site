Truffle Carbonara

$32.99
(4.2 • 127 reviews)
Limited Time Chef's Special Contains Eggs Premium

Description

Our signature Truffle Carbonara features house-made fettuccine pasta tossed with crispy pancetta, farm-fresh eggs, and aged Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese. The dish is elevated with generous shavings of premium black truffle, creating an indulgent and aromatic experience that embodies the essence of Italian luxury dining.

Prepared using traditional Roman techniques, each serving is crafted to order ensuring the perfect creamy texture without the use of cream. The pasta is cooked al dente and finished tableside with the warm egg mixture, creating a silky sauce that coats every strand.
